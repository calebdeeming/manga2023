Whether you are looking for The Game That I Came From Chapter 14 you are coming to the right place. The Game That I Came From , it is a Manga/Manhwa/Manhua translated in English, written by COMING SOON This Comic is About If you max out your level in-game, you can get heaven-defying powers in real life?! Player Ling Ce finally obtained this reward through his hard work but ended up receiving the most useless skills. Just as he was mourning his luck, a mysterious power brought him back to three years in the past. With the experiences and memories of his previous life, how many different wonderful things can he bring to his new life?
